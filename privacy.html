Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Dragon Ink and Thread (“we,” “us,” “our”) operates dragoninkandthread.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide directly — name, email address, shipping address, and phone number (if given) — when you place an order, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us.
  • Order information — what you purchased, order value, and order history.
  • Payment information — we do not store your card details. When checkout is live, payments are processed securely by Stripe; your card information goes directly to Stripe and never touches our servers. See Stripe’s privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Basic site usage data — such as pages visited, via standard analytics tools, if enabled.

How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and ship your order, and to contact you about it.
  • To send you our newsletter, if you opt in — you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.
  • To respond to questions or customer service requests.
  • To improve the shop and understand what pieces people are interested in.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third Parties We Work With

  • Stripe — payment processing (once checkout is live)
  • Web3Forms & Gmail — contact form delivery
  • Buttondown — newsletter signups and email delivery
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Wix — domain registration and DNS
  • Google Fonts — website typography

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Cookies

We don’t use tracking or advertising cookies. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics — a privacy-friendly, cookieless service that counts page views without setting cookies, collecting personal data, or tracking you across other sites. Our site may also use basic browser storage to remember what’s in your basket while you browse. If we add other cookie-based features in the future, we’ll update this policy.

Your Choices

  • You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time.
  • You can request a copy of the personal information we have about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing us at dragoninkandthread@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we’ll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at dragoninkandthread@gmail.com.

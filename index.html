Our Story

Made by hand, one stitch at a time

Dragon Ink and Thread is a small, soon-to-open Texas business born from a love of fabric, color, and the little handmade things that make everyday life feel special.

Every piece is cut, sewn, and finished by hand in small batches — so no two are exactly alike. We're just getting started, and we can't wait to share what we've been making.

  • ✦ Handmade in small batches
  • ✦ Quality fabrics, thoughtful details
  • ✦ Locally made in Texas

Our Story

Every great adventure begins with a single chapter

For me, that chapter started in an unexpected place.

After becoming a mother and navigating postpartum depression, I found myself searching for something that felt like me again. I needed a creative outlet — a place to slow down, work with my hands, and rediscover the joy of making something beautiful. What began as a hobby soon became a passion, and before long, a dream was taking shape. That dream became Dragon Ink and Thread.

Books have always had a way of transporting me beyond the ordinary — from the moment I first opened Twilight to getting lost in the world of Throne of Glass, I fell in love with stories that turned everyday life into something magical. They reminded me that even the simplest moments can hold adventure, comfort, and hope. That's the feeling I want to share through everything I create.

If Dragon Ink and Thread existed in another world, you'd find it tucked inside a tiny ivy-covered cottage on the edge of a bustling city — flowers blooming along the stone path, sunlight spilling through old windows, every shelf filled with handcrafted treasures waiting for someone to take them home. The kind of place where you're greeted warmly, encouraged to stay awhile, and leave feeling just a little lighter than when you arrived. That's the feeling behind every stitch I sew.

Each piece is made in small batches with real attention to detail — I never rush the process or cut corners, because I believe handmade should mean something. When a fabric sells out, its story becomes part of the shop's history, making room for the next chapter to begin.

More than anything, I hope my creations help you feel comfortable being yourself. Whether you're headed to your favorite bookstore, spending the afternoon at a café, running errands, or just enjoying a quiet moment at home — I hope your Dragon Ink and Thread piece adds a little whimsy to your day, and reminds you that ordinary moments can still feel magical.

This shop isn't about perfection. It's about finding joy in creativity, celebrating individuality, and holding onto a little quiet magic in everyday life.

Thank you for supporting a dream that began as a path toward healing, and letting it become part of your story too.

Welcome to the Dragon's Nest. There's always room for one more adventurer.

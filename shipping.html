Shop Policies

Shipping & Returns

Processing Time

Every piece here is made to order, by hand, in small batches — so please allow 5–10 business days for your item to be cut, sewn, and finished before it ships. During busier seasons (holidays, restocks) this may run a little longer, and I’ll always note any delays at the top of the shop.

Shipping

  • We currently ship within the U.S.
  • Orders ship via USPS, and you’ll receive a tracking number by email once your order is on its way.
  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Processing time and shipping time are different — processing happens first, then your package travels. Total time from order to delivery is usually 7–14 business days.

Returns & Exchanges

Because each piece is handmade to order, we handle returns a little differently than a big-box store:

  • Ready-to-ship items (if noted as in-stock) may be returned within 14 days of delivery, unused and in original condition, for a refund or exchange.
  • Made-to-order and custom items are final sale, since they’re cut and sewn specifically for your order. If something isn’t right, please reach out — we’ll always try to make it right.
  • If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo, and we’ll send a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

How to Start a Return

Email dragoninkandthread@gmail.com with your order number and the reason for your return. We’ll get back to you within 1–2 business days with next steps.

Questions

If anything about your order doesn’t feel right, please just reach out. Dragon Ink and Thread is a small, one-person shop, and I read every message myself.
